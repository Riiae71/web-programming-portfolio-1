CAKE POP RECIPE

Cake pops are bite-sized treats made by crumbling cake and mixing it with frosting. These balls are then coated with chocolate or candy melts and can be decorated as desired.

Ingredients

  • Cake Mix
  • Egg
  • Water
  • Oil
  • Nutella or Any Chocolate
  • Milk

Steps

  • Pour cake mix into a bowl.
  • Add eggs, water, and oil according to the instructions.
  • Mix the ingredients to form a thick batter.
  • Cook the batter in your cake pop maker for about 30-40 seconds.
  • Check the pop by poking one to ensure itâ€™s cooked through.
  • Prepare the frosting with Nutella and milk.
  • Dip your cake pops into the frosting and decorate as desired.
  • Enjoy your delicious cake pops!
